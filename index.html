Our internet and data usage add up to 2% of C02 emissions worldwide – this is due to the electric power we use to keep them running. To set this figure in perspective: this is the same as the entire airline industry! And the number is growing. As shown during the pandemic, our digital legacy will grow to 5 – 7% in the future. We need to stop this. And that’s easy!

did you know

It’s easy to stop growing our own digital footprint. Streaming your favorite series in high definition, usages 86% more energy than enjoying it in standard settings. Turning off your camera during an online meeting saves 96% of carbon emissions. We need to be aware that our digital usage costs energy and therefore: CO2 emissions.
